Collection 002

The Conflict of Tension
Overview / Details

Tension breeds evolution. The tessellation of ideas woven crudely together, abruptly and spontaneously without denial, sense or order. At the point of culmination there is a balance. An outcome. A compromise. One that completes the passage towards a newly created form, thought or identity.

Collection 002 is the progression of our first collection’s workwear. Six of our original shirtings were selected and created anew. Each pattern was reinterpreted as it went through a series of rewrapping. The assemblage of different forms creating a new whole. Every line. Every seam. Every button. Every frayed edge. All elements have purpose. Carefully selected fabrics present a luxurious, tactile experience while carefully considered construction provides for a personalized fit. We envision these shirts being worn alongside many hard-working items in your closet.

"We use 100% Japanese cotton poplin with extra long staple yarn for our shirts so that it's softer and stronger, yet still has a crisp hand feel. It's a densely woven fabric which is why it’s so crisp. What I like the most about this fabric is that it reminds me of a higher count Egyptian cotton bed sheet. I want the wearer to feel the most comfortable as if they're wearing their favorite bed sheet wrapped around their body. The feeling of complete ease at home and in your bed. That is luxury to me."


— Ji Cartman
RUDIMENT founder + creative director
