Collection 002

The Conflict of Tension
Overview / Details

RUDIMENT’s first collection demonstrates the definition of RUDIMENT - the evolution. Focused on Process, Progress and Perspective the collection explores the concept of the garment and it's construction.

No design is created flat.

A single pattern was developed that evolved into each look. Each RUDIMENT garment was then draped, taken apart, recreated, meticulously frayed and diligently sewn to completion. Every seam. Every line. Every button. Every frayed edge. All elements have purpose. We present elegance as an imperfection. As a collective these details tell the greater story of the individual.

We use 100% Japanese cotton poplin with extra long staple yarn for our shirts so that it's softer and stronger, yet still has a crisp hand feel. It's a densely woven fabric which is why it’s so crisp. What I like the most about this fabric is that it reminds me of a higher count Egyptian cotton bed sheet. I want the wearer to feel the most comfortable as if they're wearing their favorite bed sheet wrapped around their body. The feeling of complete ease at home and in your bed. That is luxury to me.


— Ji Cartman
RUDIMENT founder + creative director
